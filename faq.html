Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about MAGNE.AI project, technology, and ecosystem.

What is MAGNE.AI?

MAGNE.AI is a Web3-native AI smartphone and blockchain infrastructure project. It combines secure mobile hardware, MAGNE L1 (the base blockchain network), M Hash L2 (the Layer 2 scaling network), and DePIN (decentralized physical infrastructure network) compute participation into one unified ecosystem.

Is MAGNE.AI a phone company, blockchain project, or AI project?

MAGNE.AI is an integrated project that spans all three areas:

• Hardware: MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 is a smartphone designed for digital asset ownership and secure Web3 access.

• Blockchain: MAGNE L1 and M Hash L2 provide the decentralized infrastructure layer.

• AI + DePIN: Future device-based participation programs will enable AI compute contribution and network participation.

The project is designed to integrate these components into one seamless ecosystem.

What is MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1?

MAGNE.AI Phone Gen1 is designed as a Web3-native AI smartphone integrating hardware-secured wallet access, dual-space architecture, NFC encrypted asset backup, Android 15, Google GMS compatibility, and privacy-sensitive on-device AI workflows.

• Hardware-secured wallet with TEE and CC EAL6+ secure element architecture

• Private dual-space system that separates Web2 and Web3 environments

• NFC encrypted asset backup via the HYPERSEED 72B NFC Backup Card

• Android 15 operating system with Google GMS compatibility

• On-device AI capabilities with privacy-sensitive workflows

Full specifications and availability information will be announced through official channels.

What are MAGNE L1 and M Hash L2?

MAGNE L1 is the EVM-compatible base blockchain network designed for native Web3 applications, asset ownership, and ecosystem coordination. The testnet is currently live.

M Hash L2 is an OP Stack-based Layer 2 network designed for scalable smart contract execution, lower transaction costs, and developer experimentation. The testnet is currently live.

Both networks are in testnet phase. Mainnet launch is in planning and development.

Is the MAGNE mainnet live?

No. Both MAGNE L1 and M Hash L2 are currently operating on testnet. The mainnet is in planning and development. Specific timelines for mainnet launch will be announced through official channels when confirmed.

Testnet parameters may change. Do not use testnet endpoints for production purposes.

What is $MHA used for?

$MHA is the native utility token of the MAGNE.AI ecosystem. Planned utility includes:

• Gas: Transaction fees on MAGNE L1 and M Hash L2

• Staking: Participation in network validation (subject to governance rules)

• Governance: On-chain voting for protocol upgrades and ecosystem decisions

• Future utility: Device incentives, AI compute participation, and ecosystem access (in development, subject to governance approval)
