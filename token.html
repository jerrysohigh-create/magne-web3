Native Utility Token of the MAGNE.AI Ecosystem

The $MHA token is designed as a utility token powering the MAGNE.AI Web3-native AI infrastructure, including network fees, staking, governance, and ecosystem incentives.

Gas

🔒

Stake to participate in network validation and earn rewards, subject to governance rules.

Device Incentives

Participate in future governance-approved DePIN programs as a device operator, subject to program terms and eligibility rules.
