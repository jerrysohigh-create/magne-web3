MS2 Campaign Token

MS2 is a campaign participation token — not a fixed-income product

Token What is MS2?

MS2 is a campaign participation token distributed to Session2 participants. It may be traded on PancakeSwap, staked for campaign weight, or held for future utility.

Trade Market Availability

MS2 can be traded on PancakeSwap V2. Initial liquidity of 100,000 USDT provides reasonable market depth. Part of campaign funds may be used for MS2 buyback and burn.

Stake Staking Utility

Stake MS2 to increase your future MHA allocation eligibility. Staking determines campaign weight, not guaranteed returns. All staking is subject to TGE rules.

⚠️ No Fixed Value

MS2 is a campaign token with no guaranteed value. Its market price may be volatile. MS2 staking does not represent a savings or fixed-income product.
