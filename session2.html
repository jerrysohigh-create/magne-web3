MS2 Campaign Token

MS2 is a campaign participation token — not a fixed-income product

Token What is MS2?

MS2 is a campaign participation token distributed to Session2 participants. It may be traded on PancakeSwap, staked for campaign weight, or held for future utility.

Trade Market Availability

MS2 may have market trading functionality through supported liquidity pools based on final launch arrangements. Planned initial liquidity is up to 100,000 USDT equivalent, subject to final launch conditions and official announcements.

Stake Staking Utility

Stake MS2 to increase your future MHA allocation eligibility. Staking determines campaign weight, not guaranteed returns. All staking is subject to TGE rules.

⚠️ No Fixed Value

MS2 is a campaign token with no guaranteed value. Its market price may be volatile. MS2 staking does not represent a savings or fixed-income product.

MS2 ≠ MHA

MS2 and MHA do not have a fixed conversion ratio. MS2 may only be used to calculate campaign staking weight and potential MHA allocation eligibility. Final results are subject to TGE rules, eligibility checks, and applicable restrictions.

Referral and Anti-Abuse Rules

  1. Referral rewards apply only to one-level direct referrals. No second-level, third-level, or multi-level referral reward exists.
  2. Referral relationships cannot be changed after the invited participant completes the first eligible entry.
  3. Official default entry links do not generate referral rewards and do not count toward leaderboard ranking.
  4. Market-purchased MS2, secondary market trading volume, or externally transferred MS2 do not generate direct referral rewards.
  5. MS2 purchased from the market by invited participants cannot unlimitedly increase the inviter's Community Contribution Value.
  6. MS2 staking amount counted toward an inviter's Community Contribution Value is capped at the amount of MS2 released to that invited participant through eligible campaign entries.
  7. Self-referral, bot activity, wash participation, circular funding, Sybil behavior, or other suspicious conduct may lead to reward cancellation and loss of eligibility.
  8. Large rewards, leaderboard eligibility, and pool allocation may be subject to additional eligibility review, KYC/AML requirements where applicable, and regional restrictions.

MS2 Burn Mechanism

MS2 is designed as a campaign token with a clear lifecycle

1 Buyback & Burn

A portion of each 10 USDT campaign entry may be allocated to the MS2 Buyback & Burn Pool. Execution depends on market conditions, liquidity status, campaign progress, compliance considerations, and official announcements.

2 Unreleased MS2

MS2 that has not been released through user participation after the campaign ends may be burned or permanently locked.

3 Unclaimed MS2 Rewards

Unclaimed MS2-based campaign rewards may be burned after the claim period ends.

4 Risk-Control Forfeitures

MS2 forfeited due to bot activity, wash participation, self-referral abuse, Sybil behavior, or other rule violations may be burned or permanently locked.

5 Optional Referral Change Fee

If referral change is enabled, a 500 MS2 fee may be charged and burned. This feature is optional and subject to final campaign rules.

⚠️ Buyback and burn actions are not guaranteed price support and do not represent a commitment to maintain any MS2 market value.
