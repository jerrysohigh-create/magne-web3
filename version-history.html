Website Version History & Archived Materials

Explaining the evolution of MAGNE.AI Web3 website structure, archived materials, and their relationship to current documentation.

Overview

This page explains the version history and archived materials policy for the MAGNE.AI Web3 website. The current website is a restructured Web3 infrastructure portal based on prior project materials, technical documentation, token disclosure content, and public-facing explanations. Archived materials are provided for historical transparency only.

Archived Materials Policy

  • Archived whitepaper, legal opinion, audit materials, and prior public communications are retained for transparency.
  • Archived materials may not reflect all subsequent updates, technical changes, governance discussions, regulatory developments, or website restructuring.
  • Users should refer to the current website pages for the latest public-facing description.
  • Archived materials are provided for historical reference and transparency purposes only.

Legal Opinion Scope Notice

⚠️ Important Notice

The legal opinion, if made available, was issued based on materials reviewed at the time of issuance. It should not be interpreted as a legal endorsement of all subsequent website updates, future product features, token market activity, exchange decisions, listing outcomes, or third-party conduct.

The legal opinion represents the reviewer's assessment at a specific point in time and should be considered within its original scope. Subsequent changes to website content, technical specifications, tokenomics, governance rules, or regulatory environment may fall outside the original opinion's scope.
