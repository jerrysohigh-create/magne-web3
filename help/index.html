DEX & Liquidity Basics

  1. DEX: Decentralized Exchange using smart contracts
  2. Swap: Exchanging one token for another
  3. Costs: Fees ranging from 0.05% to 1%
  4. Liquidity: Availability of tokens
  5. Liquidity Pools: Pools where tokens are deposited
  6. LP (Liquidity Providers): Users who earn fees for providing liquidity
  7. APY: Annual Percentage Yield
