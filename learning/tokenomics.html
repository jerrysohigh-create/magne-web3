Tokenomics

MAGNE uses a single-token economic model with $MHA serving all functions across Layer 1 and Layer 2 networks. The token economy is designed for long-term sustainability and alignment of all network participants.

Token Distribution

Category Allocation Description
Network Rewards 15% Distributed to miners, validators, and DePin participants
Ecosystem Fund TBD Grants, partnerships, and ecosystem development
Team & Advisors TBD Vested over time for long-term commitment
Public Sale TBD Available to community participants

Emission Schedule

To ensure sustainable inflation and reward early participants, MAGNE implements a halving mechanism:

Year Annual Emission Rate Description
Year 1 7.5% Initial network bootstrap and adoption
Year 2 3.75% Reduced emission as network matures
Year 3 1.875% Near-stable supply with minimal inflation

Fee Structure

  • Transaction Fees: Less than $0.0025 per transaction
  • DEX Fees: 0.05% to 1% depending on pool
  • Gas Fees: Dynamic pricing based on network congestion

Incentive Mechanisms

  • Staking Rewards: Validators earn proportional to staked amount
  • DePin Rewards: Device owners earn for providing compute resources
  • Liquidity Mining: LP providers earn fees and bonus $MHA
  • Developer Grants: Funding for building on MAGNE ecosystem
