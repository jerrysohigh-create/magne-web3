$MHA Token

$MHA is the native utility token of the MAGNE ecosystem. It serves as the backbone of the entire MAGNE economy, enabling various functions across the platform.

Token Utility

  • Gas Fees: $MHA is used to pay for transaction fees on both MAGNE L1 and M Hash L2 networks
  • Staking: Stake $MHA to become a validator or delegate to validators
  • Governance: Participate in on-chain voting for protocol upgrades and proposals
  • Network Rewards: Earn $MHA by providing compute resources through the DePin network
  • Access: Unlock premium features and AI services within the ecosystem

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: Fixed
  • Rewards Allocation: 15% of total supply allocated for network rewards
  • Halving Schedule:
    • Year 1: 7.5% annual emission
    • Year 2: 3.75% annual emission
    • Year 3: 1.875% annual emission

Earning $MHA

Users can earn $MHA through several methods:

  1. Mobile Mining: Contribute idle compute from MAGNE devices
  2. DePin Participation: Share bandwidth, storage, or compute resources
  3. Staking Rewards: Earn yields by staking or delegating tokens
  4. Network Validation: Run a validator node and earn block rewards
Note: MAGNE uses a single-token system, meaning $MHA handles all functions across L1 and L2 without needing separate tokens.
